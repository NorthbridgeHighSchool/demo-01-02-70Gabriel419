Gabriel LaTour

Over this past summer I went to North Conway in New Hampshire right, and on EVERY intersection or median and on every part of highway with grass there was an obcene amount of various campain signs. And not just for one candidate either. There were literally ones that said stuff like "We need to stop these communist pigs" and then ones right next to them for Black Lives Matter.

But the most bizzare part is the ammount. I cant event describe how many signs there were. How do people have the time to do that? Literally hundreds of signs. Insanity.
